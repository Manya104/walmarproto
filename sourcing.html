Ethical Sourcing, Transparent Supply Chains

At Walmart, our commitment to sustainability extends beyond the shelves. We work with trusted suppliers worldwide, ensuring ethical practices, fair labor, and minimal environmental impact. Together, we're building a supply chain that values people and the planet.

Ethical Sourcing Overview

Walmart’s sourcing model ensures compliance with environmental standards, social responsibility, and continuous improvement across suppliers. Our Supplier Code of Conduct promotes fair wages, safe working conditions, and environmental stewardship.

We embrace technology and data-driven insights to track product origins, reduce waste, and optimize logistics. Our blockchain initiatives increase visibility, helping customers make informed, sustainable choices.
